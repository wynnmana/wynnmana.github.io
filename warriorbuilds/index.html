Explanations
Elemental abbreviations (ETW, TFA, ...)
  • To easily find out which skill points a build is using we use letters:
    • E for strength (Earth)
    • T for dexterity (Thunder)
    • W for intelligence (Water)
    • F for defence (Fire)
    • A for agility (Air)
  • They always are written in the same order as the skill points in the compass: ETWFA
  • For example ETW is a build using strength, dexterity and intelligence.
  • It is irrelevant which elemental damage you deal. If you use a weapon that does ETW (Earth Thunder Water) damage in an ETWF build it's still an ETWF build. Similarly, you do not have to deal rainbow damage in an ETWFA aka rainbow build.
Too many skill points!
  • When a build says it requires too many skill points that means you have to get those skill points from tomes (fabled weapon mastery tomes).
Upper scream surfing / Upper bash surfing
  1. Take aerodynamic charge and whirlwind strike
  2. Initialize your cycle with upper charge
  3. Cycle upper scream / upper bash (or at really high cps upper scream bash) until the fight is over.
    Aerodynamic charge doesn't end until you touch the ground and whirlwind strike prevents you from touching the ground since it always pushes you up. Because of this you can constantly fly with aerodynamic charge's speed while cycling upper and either scream or bash.
Crafted tierdrop ring
  • These rings can be used to get super slow attack speed which is needed for effortless mana steal and life steal usage as well as easy powder special charging.
  • You only use them for their negative attack speed tiers and negative stats don't decay on crafteds, so you always keep them at 0 durability, never repair them.
